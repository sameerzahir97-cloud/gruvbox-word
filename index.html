    Gruvbox Word

    A calm, fast, offline-first writing app with the warm Gruvbox palette and JetBrains Mono. It blends a familiar word-processor toolbar with live Markdown, so you can write however you think — then export anywhere.

    Why it's nice

    • Hybrid editing — click the toolbar, or type Markdown (# , **bold**, - ) and watch it format live.
    • Works offline — install it like an app; your words never leave your device.
    • Export everywhere — Word (.doc), Markdown, HTML, plain text, and PDF.
    • Many documents with automatic saving and a live outline.
    • Light & dark Gruvbox themes, plus a distraction-free zen mode.
    • Checklists, focus mode & a “/” menu — calm power features that stay out of your way.
